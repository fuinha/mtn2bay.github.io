About Mike

About Me

I am a builder and outdoor enthusiast. I love developing creative solutions and turning ideas into reality. I am always up for a good adventure and challenge. Living a healthy and productive lifestyle while pushing to achieve new levels is important to me.

My academic years were spent studying web development and media production. This experience along with a keen attention to detail allows me to create excellent products.

On my days off you can find me in the mountains or on the bay. I'm passionate about backcountry skiing, rock climbing and saltwater fishing. During the summers I have been guiding fisherman on our very special Casco Bay.
